Cherry

Thinning the fruit is not necessary for cherry trees, as they typically thin naturally in early summer.

Corn

Corn plants prefer daytime temperatures of 75 to 80 degrees F and 65 to 70 degrees F during the night.

Grape

Apply water only to the root zone. Avoid getting grape foliage wet as this can encourage many grape diseases.
